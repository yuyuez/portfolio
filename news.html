The Challenges: to build an interactive newsfeed website

The Scope: two weeks; four front-end programmers.

My work: to set up firebase as an online database and to write algorithm to generate card colors. As the front-end web programmer, my job was to set up an online database by using Firebase. I also wrote algorithm for generating the color of cards, indicating the emotion readers have toward the news.

Description: ReactNews is an interactive newsfeed website that takes in news by sources and topics from NewsAPI. Readers can choose the topic and the source they want to read news from and click on the emojis at the bottom of each news to express how they feel about the news. There is also a “statistic” page that reports the news with highest votes for each emotion in a table format. The meaning of this website is for people to read news that they are interested in. Readers can then express how they feel about the news and see how other people in the world feel about the news. Readers can send emails to express their opinions to their state representatives by clicking on the email button on each news card and searching by zip codes. Politicians can come to this website to see how the public feels about a new policy that just comes out. Data analysts can gather information from the “statistics” page and analyze economic trends as well.
